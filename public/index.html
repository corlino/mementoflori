A simple, guided process

From the moment you reach out to the moment you see the flowers in place, every step is handled with the same care we'd want shown at the graveside of our own family.

01

Send in a request

Tell us who you're honoring, which cemetery they rest in, and the date you'd like the flowers placed — a birthday, an anniversary, a holiday, or simply a day you're thinking of them.

02

Receive a phone consultation

A member of our team calls to confirm the grave location, walk through flower and arrangement preferences, and answer any questions before anything is scheduled.

03

Flowers are arranged

We work with local florists to prepare a fresh arrangement suited to the occasion, timed so the flowers arrive at their best on the day you've chosen.

04

Placement and photo confirmation

We visit the headstone, place the arrangement with care, and take a photo on site — sent directly to you the same day, so you can see the moment for yourself.
